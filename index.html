About the Game

Think sandbox game

but it actually gives you some idea of what to do next

You can go wherever you want in Pixel Forge, but it'll still nudge you toward learning new crafting recipes.
Starting with basic tools, players unlock more advanced structures, items, and areas.
Seriously, everything counts - that random tree you chopped down? You'll probably use it for something awesome later.
